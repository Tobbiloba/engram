# Engram - Video Production Guide

> **Duration**: 60 seconds
> **Style**: Cinematic tech commercial meets developer authenticity
> **Vibe**: Blade Runner + Apple + Indie Hacker

## Shot List

#### Shot 2: Context Lost (0:03 - 0:06)

## Audio Design

**Style**: Modern cinematic electronic
**Reference**: Hans Zimmer meets Odesza
**Options**:

**Tone**: Confident but warm, not corporate
**Pacing**: Deliberate, room to breathe
**Voice**: ElevenLabs or professional VO artist

## Editing Notes

1. **Pacing**: Let shots breathe, don't rush
2. **Transitions**: Mix hard cuts with smooth morphs
3. **Text**: Minimal, only when necessary
4. **Color**: Consistent grade across all footage
5. **Sound**: Sound design is 50% of the impact
